---
title: 'Attribute Tag <a href>'
---
## Definition: 
The `<a>` tag allows your to make links, `<a>` tags are self closing meaning they do not have a closing tag. `href` is an attribute, it is added in the beginning of the `<a>` tag.

## Examples:
<CodeGroup>
```ahref example.html
<a href="https://www.google.com/">I am a link</a>
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>You can create a blank `<a>` tag, it wont result in a link, remember to include the `href` attribute.</Tip>
