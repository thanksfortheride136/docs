---
title: 'Emphasis Tag <em>'
---
## Definition: 
The Emphasis tag allows your to make text italic, `<em>` tags surround any text you want to be italics. 

## Examples:
<CodeGroup>
```html em.html
<em>I am italics</em>
```
```html emInsideParagrah.html
<p><em>I am italics inside of another tag</em></p>
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>You dont need any spaces between words and tags, think of them like parentheses.</Tip>
