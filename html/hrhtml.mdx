---
title: 'Horizontal Rule <hr>'
---
## Definition: 
The horizontal rule tag allows your to make a horizontal line on a website. `<hr>` is a self closing tag, meaning it only has an opening tag, no closing. 

## Examples:
<CodeGroup>
```html hrTag.html
<hr>
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>Nothing goes inside the `hr` tag.</Tip>
