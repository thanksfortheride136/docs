## Definition: 
The Bold tag allows your to make text bold, `<b>` tags surround any text you want to be bold. 

## Examples:
<CodeGroup>
```html bold.html
<b>I am bold</b>
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>You dont need any spaces between words and tags, think of them like parentheses.</Tip>
