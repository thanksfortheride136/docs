title: 'Heading Tags <h1>, <h2>..'
---
## Definition: 
The heading tag allows your to make a heading on a website, there are several different sizes of headings, ranging from large to small: `<h1>, <h2>, <h3>, <h4>, <h5>, <h6>`. 

## Examples:
<CodeGroup>
```html bigHeading.html
<h1>I am the biggest heading</h1>
```
```html smallHeading.html
<h6>I am the biggest heading</h6>
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>You dont need any spaces between words and tags, think of them like parentheses.</Tip>
